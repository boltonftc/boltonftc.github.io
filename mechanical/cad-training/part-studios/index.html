CAD Training — Lesson 2

Introduction to Part Studios

This is Lesson 2 of the Bolton FTC summer CAD training series. In this lesson you'll become familiar with the tools inside OnShape's Part Studio modeling environment — the workspace where you'll create every 3D part you ever design. Part Studios are where sketches become solid objects, and where you'll spend most of your time as you learn to build real robot components.

Work through the full Introduction to Part Studios course on learn.onshape.com, then complete all five exercises using the reference videos below if you get stuck.

Need help logging in or finding the course? See Lesson 1 — it walks through login and navigating to learn.onshape.com step by step.

💡 Key Idea
A Part Studio is OnShape's 3D modeling workspace. You create sketches, then use features like Extrude, Revolve, and Fillet to turn those sketches into solid 3D geometry. Every feature is recorded in the Feature List on the left — you can go back and edit any step at any time without redrawing.
✅ What to Do
  1. Open the Introduction to Part Studios course on learn.onshape.com.
  2. Work through all the lessons in order — watch the videos and complete all knowledge checks.
  3. Complete all five exercises listed below. Use the reference videos if you get stuck on any of them.
  4. Mark the course complete before moving on.
💜 Hint
If your model doesn't look right, check the Feature List first. OnShape shows errors directly on the feature that has a problem. Click the warning icon to see what's wrong — most issues are a missing dimension or a sketch that isn't fully closed.
