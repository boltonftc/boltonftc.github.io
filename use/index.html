This page explains how Bolton Robotics FTC educational content may be reused. Our goal is to share useful FTC materials while keeping clear attribution to the original source.

Allowed Use (With Attribution)

  • You may copy, adapt, and reuse this site's original educational content for non-commercial educational purposes.
  • If you reuse content, you must provide visible attribution to Bolton Robotics FTC.
  • Attribution should appear near the reused content or in an obvious credits section.

Required Attribution Text

Use this format (or equivalent wording):

Source: Bolton Robotics FTC — https://boltonftc.github.io

A direct link to the original page is preferred when possible.

Not Covered / Third-Party Content

  • Some pages include third-party links, logos, or media. Those items remain subject to their original owners' terms.
  • If a specific file or page says otherwise, that page-specific notice controls for that content.

No Warranty

Educational content is provided as-is. Verify details against official FIRST, FTC, REV, goBILDA, and competition documentation before making competition-critical decisions.
